Doombots

ECE 2031 Digital Design Laboratory

Fall 2016

The goal of this team project is to design and program a modified AmigoBot using Assembly(MIPS) and Quartus II program to achieve the objective of detecting and locating objects in an arena. This project included a technical report as well as a team presentation. (Both are attached below for reference.) Our team struggled in achieving the goal but we learned a lot about problem-solving and team synergy along the way. In the end, our robot did not completely achieve the goal of object location and detection. We only tagged half of the objects placed in the arena. We analyzed our errors after the demonstration and recorded ways that we could improve the project. Some reports from the individual labs during the semester are also linked below.
